What is it?

govCMS is an open source web content management and cloud hosting service, designed for government websites across Australia.



It takes care of most privacy, security and record management obligations, and comes standard with 24/7 monitoring, disaster recovery and threat prevention.

Standard website templates are available for use, or agencies can choose to create something more customised. A hosting-only service is an option for those that need more control.

govCMS is run by the Australian Government using private sector partners. It is available to all tiers of government and promotes sharing and re-use across agencies.

Philosophy

Open

govCMS uses open source technology (Drupal) paired with an open cloud platform provided by Acquia. This commitment to an open approach enables people across government and beyond to come together and contribute to making govCMS better.

Driven and Adaptable

govCMS is created for government, by government, and is driven by the needs of agencies and their end users. If govCMS doesn’t have something you need, tell us – chances are there are other agencies that need the same thing! govCMS believes in constant iteration to meet the changing needs of its users.

Shared

govCMS supports the “create once, use often” principle and encourages agencies to share any new features created to support their sites. For example, the Department of Communications recently developed a “Have your say” consultation module that is now available to all govCMS users for no extra charge. No more duplication of cost, time and effort, and new features become available to agencies that might not have had the means to purchase them individually.
